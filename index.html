Introduction (About Me)


Hello, I am Valley/Val, my friends call me Jeb (toki pona la, mi jan Wali). I am just some random Canadian on the internet. You may want to check out my other social media. I like videos, gaming, chatting, browsing, furries UwU, random trivia, my SO, and a whole lot of other things. BTW, I'm not too familiar with HTML/CSS, so do keep that in mind if something looks broken on this site.

Often at night, I have the most random thoughts. I write these thoughts down to look at again in the morning, when I'm able to think more clearly. Once I look them back over, I clean up the mess of words, and if I like what I see, I then set whatever it is to my status for a bit. If you want to see a list of what I have had in the past, click here. I like making them, it's a fun little project that I have going on that doesn't take that much effort. I would recommend other people to pick this up, who knows what you'll come up with? I hope someone actually ends up creating some big project from this, instead of just small quotes like mine.

There are quite a few games that I've played. If you want to see a list of the games that I think are worth shouting out, check it out here. There are so many games I want to play, but haven't yet because I don't have the time with the priority. I want to know some how people can game twenty-five hours a day, eight days a week. Meh, I guess it just means that I have a life. Living the game called life is a whole different level... BTW you just lost The Game.

I like to program. I typically use Visual Basic, since that's what my job has me use (Excel macros). I do know some other, high level languages like Batch, Python, and some JavaScript, but I don't use them as often. On the other end of the spectrum, I like to play around with Turing tarpits, such as Brainf*ck and some others that I've made myself. I haven't made any really big projects, only small programs or terminal-based games. Before you ask about it, no, my brain is too smol for any "real" languages, such as C.

IDK where to put this other than here. This just contains a few forewarnings on how I am, so you can hopefully have some patience with me. I know that this doesn't excuse me from one of these behaviours, but I have been working on it, so who knows?
